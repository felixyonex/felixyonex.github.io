About Me

Greetings!

Once I was a chemical engineer in SINOPEC. I had a master degree in chemical engineering and a bachelor degree in applied chemistry.

Driven by interest towards information technology and design, I began to learn HTML, CSS and JavaScript. Thanks to the open sources, I’ve been able to continuously learn new techniques in web development. Years of experience in academic research gives me the capacity of quick learning and critical thinking.

During the process, I found myself increasingly fascinated in this field. Why not choose a career that I truly love? So, I decided to take a leap of faith.

I finished my study of front-end technology systematically in IT Cast Education and Baidu Institute of Front-End Technology. I gained valuable experience in web development from projects finished on my own and in collaboration with others developers. I plan to dig in front-end tech and expand my skillset to back-end and UI-design as well.

Currently I’m building an academic search website designed for the undergraduate students.

If you are looking for a front-end developer, feel free to contact me!
