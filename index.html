I have a great sense of fulfillment when I finished a project on my own and debugged the code to make the program functioning. I find myself in a positive feedback loop which has been the core drive of my continuous learning.

I plan to dig in front-end techniques and expand my scope to back-end as well. Interdisciplinary knowledge is also a huge attraction to me. It diversifies my knowledge repository and brings me new horizon for this world.
